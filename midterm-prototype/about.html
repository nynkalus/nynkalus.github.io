About

Who

is ARC? We are Athletics & Recreation Centre. This is an exciting venture for Algonquin Community. This facility will provide our students with a place to break through perceptual barriers in a welcoming safe, inclusive and inviting environment for students of all backgrounds, cultures and abilities to sparka movement

What

is this going to cost me? This facility will be included in your activity fee. All fee-paying students can use and participate in all activities in the building. As for the facility, it will cost Algonquin College $48.8 million

Where

will the ARC bulding be? The ARC building will be located northeast of the Student Commoins building opposite of residence. The facility will be an estimated 100,00 square feet! Connected to Student Commons by a "Plus 15" Pedestrian Link.
